### 2. Indian Web Agency (Small/Mid)

Agencies in tier-2 cities like Nagpur charge ₹50,000–3,00,000 for similar scope. Specific benchmarks:

| Agency Type | Typical Quote | What You Get |
|-------------|:------------:|--------------|
| Local Nagpur agency | ₹40,000–80,000 | WordPress or basic React site, template-based, no 3D, no animations |
| Mid-tier Indian agency | ₹1,00,000–2,50,000 | Custom React/Next.js, basic animations, CMS integration |
| Premium Indian agency | ₹2,50,000–5,00,000+ | Custom design, advanced animations, 3D, strategy consulting |

**What most agencies at the ₹40,000–80,000 range deliver:**
- WordPress or Wix template site
- No custom animations
- No 3D elements
- No Google Sheets integration (they'd push you to buy a CMS)
- No business consulting or strategy documents
- Generic responsive design

**What this project includes that they don't:**
- Three.js 3D animation (scroll-driven)
- GSAP + Framer Motion premium animations
- Custom editorial typography
- Google Sheets as free CMS (saving recurring CMS costs)
- Business strategy + consulting + multiple research documents
